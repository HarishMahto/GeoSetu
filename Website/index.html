About GeoSetu

GeoSetu is an AI-powered FRA Atlas and WebGIS-based Decision Support System (DSS) for monitoring the implementation of the Forest Rights Act (FRA). It digitizes legacy claim data, maps assets using satellite imagery, and connects tribal communities to relevant government welfare schemes.
